Home Care Can help you live the life you want!

We provide care in the care of your own home with Empathetic, professional and competent staff who Support and care tailor-made for you whilst promoting your dignity and independence. We respect your choices and preferences at all times

We offer a variety of home care solutions. Whatever your needs, we can guarantee that an ASCENTIA Carer will be by your side.

We help you stay calm and oriented by making dressing and grooming easier. We're trained to help with memory loss, manage behavior and sleep problems and encourage activities that are both stimulating and enjoyable.

We offer temporary care when the primary caregiver isn't available. This can be covering for a professional care worker, or for a family member who acts as the primary caregiver.

We provide care through the night, checking pupils at regular intervals according to their care plan. While our carers are motivated and confident to work alone, they are still able to recognise when to summon help. We respond promptly to calls for assistance while respecting, at all times, the privacy, dignity and choice of the person.

ASCENTIA Support workers work with people with a learning disability and autistic people to promote independence and wellbeing, and they sometimes provide direct physical help when called for.

We help people to keep living in their home, allowing them to always feel safe and comfortable. Offering care that would be usually given by a member of the close family. Not including the duties of a trained nurse. ASCENTIA keeps a person's bespoke care plan in mind to ensure their independence, comfort and health.

Responsible for providing medical care to patients registered with the hospice.

We help run your errands so you won't have to.
